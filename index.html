The Batter Bar

Crafted with Care, Batter to Perfection

𝕆𝕦𝕣 𝕊𝕥𝕠𝕣𝕪 :

A 23 year old student’s budget but big dreams, I launched Batter Bar — a waffle cloud kitchen built on passion, hustle, and crispy perfection. With low investment and a high-margin product, waffles were the perfect choice. I started small, mixing batter in a compact kitchen and delivering joy through Swiggy and Zomato and delivered more than one lakh rupees/ sales within 2 months when we started. Today, Batter Bar serves handcrafted, flavor-packed waffles that people love — all made fresh, all made with heart. This isn’t just a brand. It’s proof that with the right mindset, you don’t need much to start — just a good idea and the will to make it happen.
